What is ObscurA?

ObscurA is an online crypt hunt, where players crack ciphers, codes and puzzles - clues for which are all over the web.

Fire up your inner sleuth and get ready to go down the rabbit hole once again. All means are allowed and encouraged. ObscurA will not only require your knowledge but also reasoning and resourcefulness on the internet.
